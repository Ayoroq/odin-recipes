Chicken and Shrimp Pasta

Description

This recipe describes how to make Chicken and Shrimp Pasta based on personal taste and twist

I've always enjoyed pasta since I was a kid and over the years, I have enjoyed my variations.

This recipe is describes how to make one of my favourite pasta variations

This current recipe serves one but can be adjusted accordingly

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Pasta (Any kind) First attempt can be made with Spaghetti or Fettuccine
  • 1 Chicken breast or an equivalent amount of any other part of a chicken you prefer
  • Shrimps (About 20 pieces)
  • One Small sized onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • 100 ml of Milk
  • Oil
  • Cream Cheese
  • A Pan
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut your chicken pieces and season according to your personal taste
  2. Add the oil to the pan and then fry the chicken pieces
  3. Start to cook the pasta according to instructions on the pasta container/box
  4. Set aside the chicken once done cooking
  5. Cut the onion into smaller pieces and add to the same pan the chicken was fried in on medium heat (make sure to add additional oil if needed)
  6. Once the onions is starting to get caramelised, add about a teaspoon of Garlic and Ginger and stir for about 20 seconds
  7. Add the shrimp to the same pan containing the onions and stir. Allow this to cook for about 2 - 3 minutes
  8. Once the shrimp is cooked, add the chicken to the same pot and reduce to low heat
  9. Add appropriate amount of milk and 1 spoon of cream cheese to the same pot
  10. Continue to stir this until the cream cheese is all melted and combination is thickened with the milk
  11. Add black pepper to taste
  12. You can now add your cooked pasta and a little bit of the pasta water
  13. Cook this on low heat for about 1 -3 minute making sure you're mixing at the same time
  14. Voila, you're done and it's time to serve on a plate
