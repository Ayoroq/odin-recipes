Eba

Description

This recipe describes how to make the Nigerian dish Eba.

Eba is enjoyed all over Nigeria and can be had with just about every soup available

My favourite is Eba with Egusi, but you can enjoy with Efo, Ila, Banga soup, Ewedu and many more.

Ingredients

Garri is prepared with the following ingredients

  • Garri (Cassava Flakes)
  • Water
  • A Pot, kettle, or whatever can be used to bring water to boil
  • A separate bowl where the Eba will be prepared in
  • Stirring Spoon
  • Igbako (Scoop)
  • Oil 5ml (Any, just make sure it's safe with food)

Steps

  1. Bring the water to boil in the kettle or pot
  2. Transfer some of the boiled water to the second bowl from the ingredients list
  3. Now, add the Garri gently and make sure to stir at the same time.
  4. Keep adding Garri (and stirring) until all the water has been absorbed.
  5. If the texture of portion isn't to your liking, adjust with adding appropriate amount of water and garri to reach the desired consistency
  6. Keep stirring until the garri is free of any knots
  7. Now add 3 - 5 drops of Oil on the Igbako, and spread to cover surface front and back
  8. Use the igbako to shape the eba into your desired shape.
  9. Now serve the Eba on a plate beside your choice of soup.
