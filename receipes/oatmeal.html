Oatmeal

Description

This recipe describes how to make oatmeal in my style

If there's a food I can eat everyday for the rest of my life, it's probably going to be this version of oatmeal

The meal is simple and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Oats (I personally prefer rolled oats)
  • 1 medium sized banana
  • Honey (Sufficient for one honey scepter round)
  • Mixed nuts
  • Milk (I prefer to use creamy milk from Oatly)
  • A bowl where the oatmeal will be prepared in
  • A spoon
  • Microwave

Steps

  1. Add the oats to the bowl
  2. Add the milk to the contents of the bowl, adjust the milk depending on the consistency you want your oatmeal to have
  3. Cut the banana into small pieces and add to the contents of the bowl and mix
  4. Microwave this for one minute
  5. Now add the mixed nuts and one scepter of honey to the bowl contents and mix
  6. Add back to the microwave for one minute
  7. Bring out of the microwave, mix again and add back to the microwave for30 seconds
  8. Now this is ready to eat, just allow to cool down a little as it might still be hot

    9. Note:The microwave times mentioned above depends on your specific microwave, you can adjust accordingly or choose to cook the mixture in a pot instead
